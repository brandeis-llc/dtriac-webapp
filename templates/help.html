Help on search

{% endblock %} {% block content %} You can do a simple search on the text of documents in the index just by typing in a search term: 
communication
To search for particular index fields prefix the term with the field and separate field and term by a colon: 
technology:communication
event:assume
Recognized fields are 'technology', 'person', 'location', 'organization' and 'event'. In addition, relations can be search via complex fields: 'relation.pred', 'relation.arg1' and 'relation.arg2': 
relation.arg1:communication
You can create complex queries: 
location:Italy person:markov
This query will be interpreted as an AND query. You canmake this explicit or using an OR query by adding AND or OR to the beginning of the query: 
AND location:Italy person:markov lives
OR location:Italy person:markov lives
