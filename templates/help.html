Help on search

{% endblock %} {% block content %} You can do a simple search on the text of documents in the index just by typing in a search term, replace spaces in the term with underscores: 
communication
graph_coupling
To search for particular index field prefix the term with the field and separate field and term by a colon. Fields can be complex in that they refer to sub structure in a relation: 
organization:National_Science_Foundation
technology:communication
event:connected
relation.pred:connected
relation.arg1:communication
Recognized fields are author, technology, person, location, organization, event, relation.pred, relation.arg1 and relation.arg2. For all these fields there are abbreviations, the abbreviations are the first letter for the simple fields (author etcetera) and pred, arg1 and arg2 for the relation fields: 
t:communication
pred:connected
You can create complex queries: 
location:Italy person:markov
The query above will be interpreted as an AND query. You can make this explicit by adding AND to the beginning of the query, you can also use OR to make the query an OR query: 
AND location:Italy person:markov lives
OR location:Italy person:markov lives
